Both involve complex and psychological battles that may be hard to understand, but brilliant. While Liar Game focuses solely on the games, Usogui has a unique and in a way realistic theme that gamblers need at least the equal amount of violence as his opponent has in order to secure a "fair" gamble, or else the opponent will just use violence to gain what he wants. Because of this theme, Usogui has a battle aspect while Liar Game doesn't. Both protagonists are extremely smart, and the way they outsmart the enemy is simply outstanding. Compared to Liar Game, Usogui is nerve-racking and has more tension in it's games since the characters literally put their lives on the line.  read more
