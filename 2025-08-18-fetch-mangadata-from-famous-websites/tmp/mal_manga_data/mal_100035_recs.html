Mushishi addpermalink
Mystical, magical worlds with some focus on balance of viewpoints. Witch Hat is something of an action-drama focused on balancing benefits and costs of widespread magical knowledge; Mushishi is a slice of life/mystery drama focused on balancing nature and humanity. Both also prominently feature a white-haired, one-eyed man in their late twenties. Said white-haired man goes around generally trying to help others in difficult situations while providing a lesson on their local "magic", follow the general creed of their profession but will bend it to help others/out of morality, lost an eye and their memories during their childhood, and house something that will eventually kill them and make it dangerous to settle down (figuratively for Witch Hat Atelier, literally for Mushishi). One keeps many secrets and has a major goal; the other speaks even uncomfortable truths and mostly helps others undirectedly as he travels. If art quality is important, better to experience Mushishi as an anime, though the manga is stylistic and pretty in its own right. read more
