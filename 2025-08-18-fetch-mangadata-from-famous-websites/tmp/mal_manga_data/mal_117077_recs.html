ReLIFE addpermalink
If you liked the Coming-of-Age and Human Drama elements of "ReLIFE", you might enjoy "I Want to Eat Your Pancreas"! "ReLIFE" looks at the pains that one holds from past experiences, as well as how to accept them, digest them, and grow from them. Similarly, "I Want to Eat Your Pancreas" focuses on sensitising oneself to human connections and finding the personal significance of cocooning onself in them. In both stories, we follow the journeys of those who have been given "second chances" in life, learning how to build genuine human relationships which ultimately enrich their lives.
