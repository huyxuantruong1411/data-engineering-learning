Kimi ga Shinu made Koi wo Shitai addpermalink
The overall premise of these two manga is quite similar. Both are about two opposite female characters who suddenly begin spending time together. However, a reveal about one of the two leaves the other in shock. The shocked girl must now confront the fact while trying to understand the feelings she has accumulated since becoming a pair. If you enjoy the emotional impact of the happy highs and heart-wrenching lows, you will find either of the two to be enticing. Kimi ga Shinu made Koi wo Shitai is set in a fantasy world, while Kimi to Tsuzuru Utakata is set in the modern world, so depending on your perspectives, one or the other may leave you with a greater impression. read more
