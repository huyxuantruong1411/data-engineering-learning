19 Days and SQ are some of the best school comedies I know of from manhua (even from webcomics in general). What comes across as funny and laughable also has its moments of seriousness, enough to give continuity to the characters so that they aren't completely dependent on an episodic premise. They come off as a very real story between friends and the connection made simply by being close to each other. They also have similar art, humour, and the best part, a ton of ho-yay. What more do you want? They are fast to read too I suppose.
