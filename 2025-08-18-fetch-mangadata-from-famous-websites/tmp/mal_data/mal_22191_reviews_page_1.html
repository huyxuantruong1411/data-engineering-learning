Synopsis

The young man Huey took over the old mansion of his deceased bibliomanic grand father as well as the book collection that's stored in there. But there was a condition: he also had to inherit the "Bibliotheca."

When he inspects the mansion, Huey discovers a cellar filled with piled up books. In the midst of them he comes across a black-dressed girl who's silently reading a book. She's called Darian and has a big lock hanging in front of her. She herself is the entrance to the "Bibliotheca of Dantalian" which contains the prohibited "Phantom Books."

Recommended
Preliminary (2/30 eps)
I bought this Manga due to its nice cover and yet i don't regret it.
A bit confusing i think but i got used to it while reading the first chapter.

Atmosphere of this manga? it feels a little like an drawn Stephen King book. For me, tho i am not the biggest Stephen King reader, the 2 books i read felt kind of same to this manga.

The Characters are quite interesting and got an humorous attitude even tho i got the feeling they are serious about what they want to do.

I love the Art, i don't know how to explain it but ... it just binds me, watching each image for endless seconds, it has so many little details.

If you're looking for an manga that has the potential to bind you, make you kind of addicted i think you're good at this manga. for me i cant wait the month to the next ban

sorry for my bad English i hope you understood the most. Its my first Review but i hoped you guys understood why i like this manga.

if you have any questions, feel free to ask.

Alex_Tsukito
Not Recommended
Preliminary (8/30 eps)
Honestly, I don’t know if I can finish this. It is so boring and dull, then the one line that did it for me was volume 2 chapter 8 where Hugh goes “I’m a realist. I only believe what I can see before me withy my very own eyes”. I read that and all I could think of was Makai Ouji: devils and realist. It was a manga on one of the reading challenges and it looked pretty good from the description, it’s quite slow in starting.

The first case was quite interesting, but for me it lacked originality and kinda ended without the main ... characters doing anything to help the situation. If they had gotten more involved and upped the intrigue to the reader it would have drawn us in more. It all ended quite quickly with the mains just standing there.

I hope the anime adaptation is/will be better.

I will give the art style credit though, it’s very cute and reminds me of Another or Gosick.
